R. Emmett Tyrrell Jr.

Editor-in-Chief

R. Emmett Tyrrell, Jr. is the founder and Editor-in-Chief of The American Spectator. He is the author of The Death of Liberalism, published by Thomas Nelson Inc.

Melissa Mackenzie

Publisher

Melissa Mackenzie is Publisher of The American Spectator. Melissa commentates for the BBC and has appeared on Fox. Her work has been featured at The Guardian, PJ Media, and was a front page contributor to RedState. Melissa commutes from Houston, Texas to Alexandria, VA.

Evan Maguire

Editorial Assistant

Evan Maguire is an American Spectator's Editorial Assistant and a participant in the Young Writers Program. He writes on religion, foreign affairs, and culture.

Wlady Pleszczynski

Executive Editor

Wlady Pleszczynski is the Executive Editor of The American Spectator. His work has also appeared in the Wall Street Journal and the Times Literary Supplement.

Hannah Rowan

Managing Editor

Hannah Rowan is Managing Editor of The American Spectator.

Amory Manuel

Head of Technology

Amory Manuel is Head of Technology at The American Spectator. He is a recent graduate of the University of Michigan, where he studied history and computer science while serving as the Executive Editor of The Michigan Review.
